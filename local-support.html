本地事务服务概述

全方位安家服务

Nord Capital LLC 提供全面的本地事务协助服务，帮助新移民、留学生和投资者快速适应新生活。我们了解初到异国他乡可能面临的各种挑战，因此设计了覆盖生活各个方面的服务套餐。

从银行开户、驾照办理到学校注册、税务申报，我们的专业团队将为您提供一站式解决方案。

Comprehensive Settlement Services

We provide comprehensive local support services to help new immigrants, students, and investors quickly adapt to their new life. We understand the various challenges faced when arriving in a new country.

From bank account opening, driver's license application to school registration and tax filing, our professional team provides one-stop solutions.
